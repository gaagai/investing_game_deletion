Requesting Account Deletion

You can delete your personal information (email and password provided by you) at any time through the account section of the app. Simply navigate to the rightmost button on the bottom tab navigation bar and press the 'Delete Account' button. Your account will be automatically deleted.

The information to be deleted includes the email address and password you provided at the time of registration.

Data Retention

The account will be completely deleted, and no data will be retained.
